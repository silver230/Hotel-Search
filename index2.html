BIDWOOD HOTEL

A contemporary five-star hotel in Nairobi

With a vibrant, urban spirit and a character that is deeply ingrained in the art, culture, lifestyles and cuisine of Nairobi, our luxury hotel offers more than just a place to stay. BIDWOOD SUITES

REDEFINES THE LUXURY

HOSPITALITY EXPERIENCE

Bidwood Suite Hotel is located on Karuna Road in Nairobi’s prime Westlands Area.

It is a 45 minute drive from the Jomo Kenyatta International Airport.

And 15 minutes drive to the Central Business District and a 25 minute drive to the Nairobi National Park.

The hotel is strategically located with close and easy access to prominent shopping malls in the area.

The hotel has 44 rooms distributed throughout the 7 floors with a penthouse on the 8th floor.

Located on the ground floor is Ventana Bar & Restaurant which offers international cuisine and operates round the clock.

We also cater for conferencing and events requirements. The Spa and Salon is located on the top floor which offers a variety of relaxing treatments and services.
